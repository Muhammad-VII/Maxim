Samir has a proven track record of achievement and innovation in the development and hospitality sectors. Essam Hassan joins Maxim Investment Group as “Group Chief Financial Officer” as of 2021. Essam holds a Masters of Business Administration in Corporate Finance (MBA) as well as a Certified Financial Technician (CFTe) and Certified Auditor (CA), he joins MIG after his posts as the CFO at AliBaba Group, Financial Controller and Virgin Megastore and Azadea Group in Egypt.

After spending over two decades in corporate finance, banking, retail and as well as business development, Essam has mastered corporate restructuring and transformation with a long-proven record financial leadership and corporate structures.
