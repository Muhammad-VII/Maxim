MIG Over Time

Founded in 1980 by Mr. Wagdy Karrar, the Maxim Investment Group of companies is a leader in real estate investment and development firms. At inception, MIG originally started in the construction sector, over the years it has successfully built, developed and executed several other ventures to accumulate an impressive portfolio.

MIG has expanded its activities by actively leading and participating in joint ventures with public as well as private investors, solidifying MIG as a pre-eminent hospitality and real estate investment forms. MIG is now a vertically integrated developer, investor and partner for hospitality and mixed-use real estate.
